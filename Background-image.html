Technology and Health (AI)

Positive Effects of Technology on Health

Technology has made healthcare faster and more accurate.
Doctors use advanced machines for diagnosis and treatment.
Fitness apps and smartwatches help people track exercise, heart rate, and sleep.
Technology also connects patients and doctors through online consultations.

Negative Effects of Technology on Health

Spending too much time on screens can cause eye strain and poor posture.
Lack of physical activity due to gadgets leads to health problems.
Overuse of technology can also affect mental health and sleep quality.
It is important to use technology in a balanced way for a healthy life.
